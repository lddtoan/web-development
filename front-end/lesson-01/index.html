Ingrredients in pizza soup

This pizza soup was only a matter of time. Alex and I have been enamored with making artisan-style pizza since out honeymoon to Italy year ago. We've spent a decade researching pizza dough recipes and learning from professionals, and you'll see that culmination in out next level Margherita Pizza . So what better challenge than to take all that know how and distill it down into a...soup?

This pizza soup is for when you want to experience of pizza, but none of the hassle. The broth tastes so spot on that after a spoonful you'll exclaim, "Thats taste just like pizza!" (Promise. We did.) Here are the ingrredients you need to make this healthy whole food recipe:

  • Pizza sauce
  • Canned crushed tomatoes (fire roasted if possible)
  • Vegetable broth
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Red onion, garlic, bell pepper, mushroom
  • Dried oregano and fennel seeds
